   Rock climbing is a major passion I have developed in the last few years. I was first introducd to the sport by my sister and father in high school, but I didnt fall in love until I took classes at a local outdoors store. Since then, I have spend a considerable ammount of time on the rock, and at the gym. I primarily sport climb and boulder, but also have experience leading trad both single pitch locally, and multi pitch on Seneca Rocks in West Virginia. Additionally, I have taught basic climbing lessons at the university wall for three semesters, and at a local gym for a year and a half. I am always willing to help others get into the sport, so feel free to contact me with questions, or interest insome introductory lessons.
